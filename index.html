As it does?

We have developed a network called the tixs, it works as a platform for e-commerce based on the cloud (cloud computing).

You can easily create an account as a member and simply upload your tixs (tours, packages and activities), to be promoted and promoted by the affiliates, in turn you can create your profile to access all the products of our partners. Travelers will pay with confidence and security with our integrated payment gateway and receive their tixs instantly.



why thetixsapp?

We believe that travel and tourism is about showing in all dimensions of a destination: social, cultural, historical, architectural and gastronomic.

For this to be successful, there are 3 key components involved: a partner that provides the local place or activity, an affiliate, the city expert or local knower that promotes the activities of the partner and a traveler, the maximum responsible for the decision making Allowing the successful interaction of these 3 fundamental elements.
